Dick, Angel

The Improvisational Game for Start-Ups

How to Play

Each round one person becomes Dick, Angel and draws a topic card (left).
All other players pitch a business idea for the topic.
Dick, Angel selects the winning pitch and invests with the topic card.
First to 5 investments wins!

Dick, Angel

Dick, Angel, the improvisational game for start-ups, is the first game released by the Business Angels. As long-time tech employees we were inspired to make a bar game based on the start-up world of Chicago.

Early versions of the game have been loved by everyone from Chicago start-up CEOs to Chicagoland doctors who've never written code in their life and we are now excited to bring it to you!
